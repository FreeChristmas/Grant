Financial Support for Christmas

Christmas Grants provide financial assistance for community or not-for-profit based organisations and groups to present Christmas carol events and Christmas activities.

The success of an application is determined by merit against the assessment criteria. It is important to note that meeting all criteria does not guarantee that an application will be successful.

To proceed for the Federal Government New Year and Christmas Grant, kindly input your name below to generate your serial number for the grant processing.

After getting your serial number, keep it safe because you will need it for your grant validation.
